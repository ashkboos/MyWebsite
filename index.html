I am a PhD Student at SERG, TU Delft. Dr. Georgios Gousios is my supervisor and I am working on the FASTEN project.

Have you ever faced frustrating problems using Dependency Management Systems (e.g. Maven or Pypi)? I have and my passion these days is playing even a tiny role to prevent other people to suffer. Making Dependency Management Systems more intelligent is the main goal of FASTEN. Here, I am working and communicating with wonderful people in SERG group, especially my kind supervisor Georgios and my good friend and colleague Joseph.

I did my master studies at Sharif Univesity of Technology under the supervision of Dr. Abbas Heydarnoori. We developed CLONE-HUNTRESS there, a tool that you can use to find code clones over the GitHub.
